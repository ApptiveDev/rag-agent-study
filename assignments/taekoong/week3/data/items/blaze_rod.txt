A blaze rod is an item obtained from blazes. It is required to craft brewing stands and fuel them in a powdered form, which is also required to craft eyes of ender.

== Trivia ==
As part of an official collaboration, blaze rods are included in a free add-on for the roleplaying game Dungeons and Dragons. In the game, they are worth 100 gold pieces, create light, and can be broken to create an explosion.
